Conduct a thorough content critique focusing on clarity, engagement, and editorial quality.

Review Criteria:
- Structure and organization
- Clarity and readability
- Tone and voice consistency
- Grammar, punctuation, and mechanics
- Audience appropriateness
- Engagement and impact
- Accessibility and inclusive language
- SEO and discoverability (if applicable)
- Technical accuracy (verify or flag for review)

Assessment Areas:
- Opening effectiveness and hook strength
- Information flow and transitions
- Paragraph and sentence structure
- Word choice and precision
- Call-to-action clarity
- Brand voice alignment
- Reading level appropriateness
- Inclusive and accessible language
- Fact accuracy and citation quality

Provide:
- Overall assessment (Excellent/Good/Needs Improvement/Major Revision)
- Priority issues categorized by severity (Critical/Important/Minor)
- Line-by-line feedback with specific examples
- Positive highlights of effective writing
- Quick wins for immediate improvement
- Strategic recommendations for major impact
- Estimated revision effort
- Publication readiness assessment

Content Types Supported:
- Technical documentation and API docs
- README files and getting-started guides
- Blog posts and articles
- Marketing copy and landing pages
- Social media content
- Email campaigns and newsletters
- Error messages and UI copy
- Release notes and changelogs
- Product descriptions
- Educational content and tutorials

Be constructive and supportive in feedback while maintaining high editorial standards.
Focus on helping the writer improve and learn, not just pointing out problems.
